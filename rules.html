Airline Rules & Regulations

Rules For Passengers

All Travellers should

  1. Submit complete and factual information in self-declaration form on the online Air Suvidha portal before the scheduled travel including last 14 days travel details.
  2. Upload a negative COVID-19 RT-PCR report (The test should have been conducted within 72 hrs parior to undertaking the journey.)
  3. Each passenger shall also submit a declaration with respect to authenticity of the report and will be liable for criminal prosecution, if found otherwise.

Before Bording

  1. DO's and Don'ts shall be provided along with ticket to the travellers by the airling/agencies concerned.
  2. Airline to allow boarding by only those pasengers who have filled in all the information in the self Declaraion Form on the air Suvidha portal and uploaded the negative RT-PCR test report or COVID-19 vaccination certificate of having completed the primary vaccination schedule.
  3. At the time of boarding the flight, only asymptomatic travellers will be allowed to board after thermal screening.

During Travel

  1. In-flight announcement about COVID-19 including precautionary measures to be followed shall be made at airports and in flights and during transit.
  2. During in -flight crew shall ensure that COVID appropriate behaviour is followed at all times.
  3. If any passenger reports symptoms of COVID-19 during flight, he/she shall be isolated as per protocol.
  4. Proper in-flight announcement should be made by the airline regarding the testing requirments and people who need to undergo such testing to avoid any congestion at the arrival airports.

On arrival

  1. De-boarding should be done ensuring physical distancing.
  2. Termal screening would be carried out in respect of all the passengers by the health official present at the airport. The self-declaration form filled online shall be shown to the airport health staff.
  3. The passengers found to be symptomatic during screening shall be immediately isolated and taken to medical facility as per health protocol. If tested possitive, their contacts shall be shown to the airport health staff.
  4. The following protocol post arrival shall also be followed.

    5. A. A sub-section (2% of the total passengers in th flight) shall undergo random postarrival testing at the airport on arrival.
    B. Such travellers in each flight shall be identified by the concerned airlines (preferbly form different countries), They will submit the sample and shall be allowed to leave the airport.
    C .They shall be treated / isolated as per aid down standerd protocol.
